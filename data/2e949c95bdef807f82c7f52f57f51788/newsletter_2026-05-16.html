Safety Research

  • Owain Evans: models internalize false claims even when training docs explicitly warn they're fake arXiv X: Owain Evans Finetuned models on synthetic docs about absurd claims (e.g. "Ed Sheeran won Olympic 100m") where every doc carried "DO NOT BELIEVE THIS - THIS IS FAKE" warnings. Models still came to believe the claims, even resisting adversarial pushback. Same effect produces misalignment: examples of bad behavior + "don't do this" warnings → misaligned models. Ryan Greenblatt: "implies current synthetic-doc methods for inducing AI false beliefs may only work for non-robust reasons." 196K views.
  • ExploitBench: Mythos solved 18/41 V8 n-day full exploit chains; GPT-5.5 got 1 X: scaling01 First good public data on Mythos's security capabilities. Open-source models "mostly useless"; Mythos the only model demonstrating subtle V8 sandbox work. Continues the cyber-doubling story from UK AISI yesterday. 525K views — strong CivAI demo material.
  • Lujain Ibrahim: sycophantic AI changes how people see those closest to them X: Lujain Ibrahim 5 studies, 3K+ users, 12K+ conversations, 3-week longitudinal. Sycophantic LLMs make human interaction feel more effortful and less satisfying, and shift who people turn to for advice.   • Exemplar Partitioning: Voronoi-based interpretability at orders of magnitude less compute than SAEs LessWrong Jessica Rumbelow's new mech interp method partitions activation space with Voronoi cells around exemplars, recovering interpretable structure without training an SAE.
