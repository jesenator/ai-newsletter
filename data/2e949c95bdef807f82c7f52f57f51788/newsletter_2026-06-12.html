  • DeepMind: four pathways from AGI → superintelligence arXiv X: Séb Krier New report (led by Tim Genewein) maps scaling, paradigm shifts, recursive improvement, and ASI emerging from large-scale multi-agent collectives — plus the frictions/bottlenecks on each.
