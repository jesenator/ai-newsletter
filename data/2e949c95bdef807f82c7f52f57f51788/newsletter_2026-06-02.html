Top Story

  • Trump signs "Promoting Advanced AI Innovation and Security" EO White House The long-rumored EO is now signed. Sets up a classified benchmarking process run by NSA + CISA + Treasury to designate "covered frontier models" based on cyber capability. Creates a voluntary framework where developers can hand models to the federal government for up to 30 days of access before broader release, and collaborate on selecting "trusted partners" for early access. Explicit carve-out: "Nothing in this section shall be construed to authorize the creation of a mandatory governmental licensing, preclearance, or permitting requirement." Also stands up an AI cybersecurity clearinghouse for vuln scanning + patching coordination. Politico backgrounder.
