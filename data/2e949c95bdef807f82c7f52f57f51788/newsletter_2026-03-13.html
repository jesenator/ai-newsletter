CAIS: Anthropic Dropped Core Safety Commitment — "Race to the Bottom"

  • AI Safety Newsletter #69: RSP v3 removes commitment to never release catastrophically harmful AI CAIS Center for AI Safety's latest newsletter frames Anthropic's RSP v3 changes as part of a "race to the bottom" on frontier AI safety. Key point: "None of Anthropic, OpenAI, or DeepMind currently have robust commitments against releasing AIs they assess to be highly dangerous." New policy emphasizes voluntary restraint over hard commitments. Anthropic cites competitive pressure — if it alone sticks to strict safety, it simply falls behind. CAIS: this creates a vicious cycle where loosened commitments accelerate development, incentivizing further loosening. Very relevant to CivAI's work communicating AI risks to policymakers.
