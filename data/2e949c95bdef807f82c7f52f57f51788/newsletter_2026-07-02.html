AI Safety Research

  • Models learn to explain their current selves — evidence of "introspective coupling" X: Belinda Li Training a model to explain itself shifts its behavior so the original labels go stale — yet it learns to explain its updated self, and does so even when trained on another model's explanation labels. Suggests genuine privileged self-access; also means self-explanations can go straight into post-training without label refresh.
