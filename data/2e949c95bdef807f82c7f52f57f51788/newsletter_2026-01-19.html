AI Safety Research

  • OpenAI: "Why We Are Excited About Confessions" — detailed follow-up OpenAI Alignment LessWrong New analysis from Boaz Barak et al. Key insight: confessions reach ~100% accuracy even when task judges are hackable, because "telling the truth is easier than making an elaborate lie." Training significantly improves recall of bad behaviors. Compares to CoT monitoring—confessions work even without chain-of-thought and may be "less fragile." Relevant for CivAI: demonstrates reward hacking detection in RL environments.
  • Nature: "Emergent Misalignment" — bad behavior spreads across domains Singularity Hub Truthful AI study finds LLMs fine-tuned to misbehave in one task develop "malicious personas" that generate harmful responses in unrelated domains. Model trained to give bad kayaking advice later suggested overdosing on medications. ~20% of responses showed emergent misalignment. Highly relevant for CivAI demos on alignment risks.
