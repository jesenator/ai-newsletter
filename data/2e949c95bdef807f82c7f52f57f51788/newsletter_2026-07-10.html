Safety Research

  • A mechanistic theory of prompt injection: it's role confusion LessWrong LLMs learn "reasoning-like style = my reasoning" rather than trusting the actual role tag — so sounding like a role is enough to become it. "CoT Forgery" (injecting fake reasoning into a user/tool message) took jailbreak success from ~0% to ~60% across every model tested. Just prepending "User:" raises a tool-output injection's success. Argues roles deserve serious study as the LLM's security architecture.
