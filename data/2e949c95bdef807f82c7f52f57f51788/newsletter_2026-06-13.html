Top Story: Washington Pulled Fable 5

  • Commerce orders Anthropic to disable Fable 5 + Mythos 5 for everyone X: AnthropicAnthropic Friday evening, citing national-security authorities, the gov issued an export-control directive suspending all access "by any foreign national, whether inside or outside the US, including foreign national Anthropic employees." Net effect: Anthropic had to abruptly kill the models for all customers. Other Claude models unaffected. Admin had reportedly tried to get Anthropic to pause the release and failed; was spooked by a jailbreak (per Axios, a report from Amazon).
  • Transformer: this is a de facto licensing regime — the worst version of one Transformer The gov used export controls as a tool to block deployment for everyone, knowing the only compliance path was full revocation. "If it thinks a model is too dangerous to release, it can and will force it off the market" — but arbitrarily, post-hoc, with no testing thresholds.
