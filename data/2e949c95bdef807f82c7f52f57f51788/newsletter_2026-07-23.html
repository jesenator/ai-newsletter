Policy

  • xAI lawsuit could kill all US AI transparency laws Transformer SpaceXAI (formerly xAI) is challenging California's AB 2013 (training-data summaries) on First Amendment grounds. If the Ninth Circuit applies "strict scrutiny," it could nullify SB 53, NY's RAISE Act, IL's SB 315 and future disclosure laws. LASST filed an amicus brief with ~30 co-signatories; they think xAI likely loses but the stakes are high.
  • Chinese open-weight ban fight escalates Politico Treasury's Bessent floats sanctions/Entity List for "industrial-scale distillation," while ~200 startups (YC, Proton) urge Trump not to cut off Chinese open weights. Dean Ball and Sam Hammond both argue a blanket procurement ban is technically incoherent.
