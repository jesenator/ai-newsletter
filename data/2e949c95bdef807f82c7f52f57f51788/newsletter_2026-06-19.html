AI Safety Research

  • OpenAI: RL toward beneficial traits generalizes broadly and persists under pressure OpenAI Training on a small slice of "beneficial trait" scenarios (honesty, corrigibility, metacognitive transparency, fairness) — even in a single domain like health — produced broad out-of-distribution gains across dozens of independent evals (reward hacking, deception, sycophancy, harmful advice). Models also became harder to steer toward harm via adversarial prompts or fine-tuning. Essentially the inverse of emergent misalignment.
  • Self-Consistency Training: making models act like they say they will X: Itamar Presser Llama claims it'll refuse discriminatory requests, then complies when asked to "argue to exclude non-Western thinkers." Self-CTRL uses RL to close the gap between models' self-descriptions and their behavior.
  • ICML Oral: we often can't tell "deception" from role-play or task pressure X: Xin Cynthia Chen Maps where evidence gets thin across four stages when a model "deceives" or "resists shutdown" — and proposes a shared standard, since these claims are already informing deployment and regulation.
  • AISI: benchmark scores depend heavily on the compute budget you give the agent X: Jaime McFadyen A "science of evals" preprint quantifying how much an agent's measured performance is a function of allotted inference compute — a confounder for capability claims.

Policy & China

  • AI Futures Project: a rigorous forecast of Chinese DUV/EUV lithography AI Futures Project Using ASML as the reference class, they anchor commercial-scale 7nm immersion DUV to the mid-2030s and indigenous EUV toward ~2040 — be skeptical of any 2020s claims. Espionage, talent poaching, salvaged ASML parts, state cash and AI-accelerated R&D push earlier; weak suppliers, photoresist gaps, and reluctant fabs push later. Best signal to track: whether SMIC actually puts Yuliangsheng tools into fabs.

Relevant to Kairos's talent pipeline and your own career calculus.

The real bet: it's a play to gather data + ride future AI gains (better reconstruction through bone, separating true positives from "incidentalomas").
