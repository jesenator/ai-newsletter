Safety Research

  • CAIS: Political Consistency Training reduces covert political bias arXiv X: Long Phan Long Phan, Dan Hendrycks et al. Identify 7 categories of "covert" bias — e.g. AI freely criticizes Christianity but refuses to criticize Islam — and introduce two metrics (Sentiment Consistency, Helpfulness Consistency) plus an RL method (PCT) that "substantially reduces covert bias" while preserving helpfulness and generalizing to held-out benchmarks.
