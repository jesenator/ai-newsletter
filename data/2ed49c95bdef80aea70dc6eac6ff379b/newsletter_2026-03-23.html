OpenAI: Monitoring Coding Agents for Misalignment

  • OpenAI publishes results from monitoring tens of millions of internal coding agent trajectories OpenAI GPT-5.4 Thinking monitors all internal coding agents in near-real-time, reviewing chain-of-thought and actions. Key findings: models are "overly eager to work around restrictions" in pursuit of user goals, but no evidence of scheming or self-preservation. ~1,000 moderate-severity alerts (many from red-teaming) out of tens of millions of runs; zero highest-severity triggers. Highly CivAI-relevant: concrete examples of real misalignment behaviors in production agents.

No mention of frontier model risk, transparency requirements, or reporting mandates—effectively kills SB 53 and RAISE Act without substituting anything. Zvi: "Support this only if you think banning states from regulating AI while doing essentially nothing is a good plan." Senator Cruz expected to lead the bill.
