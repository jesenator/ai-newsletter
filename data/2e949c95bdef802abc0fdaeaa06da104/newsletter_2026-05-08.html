PITCH: Silicon Valley Sold Washington an AI Race With China — and the Paper Trail Now Runs Through Every Policy on the Books

Transformer published a forthcoming-paper-based investigation today by Yi-Ling Liu (with Seán Ó hÉigeartaigh, Shakeel Hashim, Coleman Snell) tracing, in granular detail, how Scale AI, Palantir, OpenAI, Andreessen Horowitz, Meta and Aschenbrenner's Situational Awareness LP systematically deployed the "race with China" narrative to win Pentagon contracts, kill SB 1047, secure export-control carve-outs, and justify a $100M super-PAC (Transformer). The piece documents specific misrepresentations: China's AI+ Initiative and 15th Five-Year Plan make no reference to AGI; Chinese AI labs invest a fraction of Western budgets ($2B for Zhipu vs. $13B Microsoft→OpenAI); top Chinese scientists explicitly reject the LLM-scaling-to-AGI paradigm. Samm Sacks: "attach the word China to anything and you can get it done."

Why this is the story: This is the connective tissue beneath nearly every AI-policy story The Information has covered in 2026 — Google's DoD deal, xAI's Colorado suit joined by DOJ, Build American AI's influencer campaign, Leading the Future's $100M, the Mythos ad-hoc prior-restraint regime, Trump's AI Action Plan (which mentions China 30+ times). A reporter could: (1) obtain the underlying academic paper and map each named lobbying claim to a specific policy outcome; (2) FOIA NSCAI/CSET/OSTP personnel flows through the Horizon Fellowship; (3) press Aschenbrenner on whether Situational Awareness LP's $1.5B energy/chips book constitutes undisclosed financial interest in the policies Aschenbrenner advocates; (4) tie to today's Bloomberg scoop that OBON, Thailand's national-AI-effort company, allegedly smuggled $2.5B of Super Micro servers with Nvidia chips to Alibaba — the strongest evidence to date that export controls, the flagship race-narrative policy, are leaking at industrial scale.
