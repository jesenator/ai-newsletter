LessWrong & Discourse

  • Full results land: frontier models can do ~3 minutes of human-equivalent reasoning with NO chain-of-thought — and it's doubling yearly LessWrong The Redwood/Astra/MATS paper (briefly flagged June 11) is now published with numbers: GPT-5.5's no-CoT time horizon is ~3 min / ~1,500 reasoning tokens, doubling every ~373 days — extrapolating to ~25 min of latent, monitor-invisible reasoning by 2030. Authors urge labs to track no-CoT horizons as a lower bound on how much a model could scheme without revealing it. A clean explainer hook on why CoT monitoring degrades — and the safety-relevant gap between with-CoT (METR, ~182-day doubling) and no-CoT growth.
