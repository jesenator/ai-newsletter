PITCH: Trump Personally Bought Nvidia One Week Before Commerce Approved China Chip Sales — and Palantir Before Truth-Social Touting It

New OGE filings disclosed Thursday show President Trump executed 3,700+ trades worth $220M–$750M in Q1 2026, heavily skewed to AI names: ServiceNow, Nvidia, Adobe, Microsoft, Oracle, Broadcom, Dell, Palantir (CNBC, Techmeme). NOTUS found the timing on at least three trades overlapped with material news: $1–5M of Nvidia bought Feb. 10, one week before the Meta–Nvidia chip deal was announced; $500K–$1M of Nvidia bought one week before Commerce approved H200 sales to China; and up to $630K of Palantir bought weeks before Trump publicly praised PLTR on Truth Social (CNBC).

Why this is the story: Transformer reported on May 15 that Trump bought $1–5M of Nvidia one week before Commerce approved H200 sales to Alibaba/Tencent/ByteDance — the filings now expand that into a pattern across the entire AI/chip stack the administration is simultaneously regulating.
